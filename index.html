About Kalimera Imports

What does "Kalimera" mean?

Kalimera (Καλημέρα) - "Good Day" in Greek. But for us, it's more than a greeting. It's our promise to every partner: we bring good days through exceptional service, reliable sourcing, and unwavering quality at enterprise scale.

As a multi-generational family business, we carry forward a legacy of trust, integrity, and personal relationships that spans decades. We don't just wish you a good day - we deliver it through every container, every partnership, every transaction, building on the foundation our family has established in the foodservice industry.

Our Mission

Kalimera Imports serves as your global sourcing partner for foodservice and industrial markets. With family-owned values and restaurant industry DNA, we provide the same level of dedicated service to our enterprise customers that we've always delivered to smaller operations - just at container scale.

Global Presence

We travel personally to meet manufacturers, inspect facilities, and serve as your eyes, ears, and taste buds around the world. From Mediterranean traditions to modern frozen food technology, we bring you products that meet the highest quality standards.

Personal Service

Enterprise scale doesn't mean impersonal service. We maintain the same dedication and attention to detail at every level, ensuring that every partnership feels like working with a trusted family business.
